# Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization Guide

To maximize local lead generation for **Green Bay Snow Pros**, a strong Google Business Profile is essential. Even for a lead-gen test site, understanding this process is key for future scalability.

## 1. Profile Setup
- **Business Name:** Use the exact name "Green Bay Snow Pros".
- **Category:** Primary category should be **Snow Removal Service**. Secondary categories: *Landscaper*, *Property Maintenance*.
- **Address:** If you have a physical office, list it. If operating as a service-area business (SAB), hide the address and define your service area (Green Bay, Appleton, Oshkosh, Door County).
- **Phone:** Use a tracked local number (e.g., area code 920).
- **Website:** Link directly to `https://snowremoval-greenbay.com`.

## 2. Optimization Checklist
- [ ] **Service Areas:** Explicitly list all target cities and zip codes.
- [ ] **Hours:** Set to "Open 24 hours" during winter months to capture emergency searches.
- [ ] **Services:** Manually add services like "Residential Snow Plowing," "Commercial Snow Removal," "Ice Dam Removal," "Salting."
- [ ] **Description:** Write a 750-character description including keywords: *Green Bay snow removal*, *Appleton plow service*, *emergency ice removal*.
- [ ] **Photos:** Upload high-quality photos of your trucks, equipment, and before/after shots of cleared driveways. Geotag photos if possible.

## 3. Review Strategy
- **Get Reviews:** Send the review link to every satisfied lead/customer.
- **Keywords in Reviews:** Encourage customers to mention the service and location (e.g., "Best snow plow service in De Pere").
- **Respond:** Reply to all reviews within 24 hours.

## 4. Updates (Posts)
- Post weekly during winter.
- **Topics:** Storm warnings, "spots available" alerts, before/after photos, tips for preventing ice dams.
- **Call to Action:** "Call Now" or "Get Quote".

## 5. Q&A
- Populate the Q&A section with common questions (e.g., "Do you plow at 2 AM?") and answer them from the owner account.
